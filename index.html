Projects

Service Industry TipBot

During my undergrad career, I worked at a local restaurant in La Jolla called Din Tai Fung. It was something I really enjoyed and when I started my computer science journey, I thought making a tip calculator would be a fun project to work on. I wanted to make something that would be useful for my coworkers and I, so I decided to make a tip calculator where servers could log their daily tips and sales, and see how much they made that day. Addiionally, you would be filter your shifts by day or shift type to see how your averages change per shift. I used Javascript, React, and CSS to build the frontend and am currently stil in the process of adding a backend to store user data.
